## Info
The rebellion forces invading Aurelia. They are seeking to establish order and destroy all remaining magic from the lands, as they have deemed the ruling Gloridian government has resorted to a laissez-faire approach to the [[Magical Conundrum]].

Authoritarian in nature, they do not have qualms with slavery, forced curfews, and mandatory conscription.

Followers of the God, [[Thalos, God of Order|Thalos]].
